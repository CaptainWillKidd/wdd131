Discover a Timeless Classic

Chrono Trigger is a role-playing game developed by Square Enix, originally released for the Super Nintendo in 1995. The game follows the story of Crono, a young adventurer who finds himself embroiled in a quest to save the world from a catastrophic event. With its engaging story, memorable characters, and innovative battle system, Chrono Trigger has become a beloved classic among fans of the genre.

Time Travel

Embark on an epic journey through time, from the prehistoric past to the distant future. Witness pivotal moments in history, alter the course of events, and uncover the secrets of the Lavos, a mysterious entity threatening all of existence. With multiple timelines and branching paths, every decision you make shapes the fate of the world.

Unique Characters

Meet a diverse cast of heroes, each with their own rich backstories and motivations. From Crono, the silent protagonist with a heart of gold, to Magus, the enigmatic sorcerer with a dark past, every character brings something unique to the adventure. Form bonds, unlock powerful combo Techs, and discover how their fates intertwine across time.

Dynamic Combat

Experience a revolutionary battle system that combines turn-based strategy with real-time action. Unleash devastating combo attacks by pairing your party members' unique abilities. From Crono's Lightning Strike to Frog's Slurp Cut, every battle is a chance to experiment and discover new ways to triumph over your enemies.
