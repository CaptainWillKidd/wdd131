Legendary Characters

Meet these epic characters

Crono

The silent protagonist of the game, Crono is a brave and selfless hero from the Kingdom of Guardia. Armed with his katana, he embarks on a journey through time to save the world from destruction.

Marle

Princess Nadia, also known as Marle, is a spirited and kind-hearted archer. She joins Crono after accidentally traveling through time and becomes a key member of the team.

Lucca

A brilliant inventor and close friend of Crono, Lucca is a tech-savvy fighter who uses gadgets and firearms. Her intelligence and resourcefulness are invaluable to the team.

Frog

Once a human knight named Glenn, Frog is a noble warrior cursed to live as an amphibian. Wielding the legendary Masamune, he seeks to restore his honor and defeat the evil Magus.

Robo

A kind-hearted robot from the future, Robo joins the team after being repaired by Lucca. His strength and advanced technology make him a valuable ally in battle.

Ayla

The fierce leader of the Ioka Tribe in the prehistoric era, Ayla relies on her brute strength and agility. She is a fearless warrior with a strong sense of justice.

Magus

A mysterious and powerful sorcerer, Magus is initially an antagonist but later joins the team. His dark magic and tragic past make him one of the most complex characters in the game.
